Germany Tours

A land of vibrant cities, fairytale castles, and scenic landscapes, Germany offers a diverse range of experiences. Our tours cover the very best of this dynamic country.

Major Cities

From the edgy art scene of Berlin to the traditional beer halls of Munich, Germany's cities are hubs of culture, history, and innovation.

  • Berlin: Explore the capital's rich history, from the Brandenburg Gate to the Berlin Wall Memorial.
  • Munich: Discover the heart of Bavaria, famous for Oktoberfest, the English Garden, and its proximity to the Alps.
  • Hamburg: Experience Germany's "Gateway to the World" with its impressive harbor, historic warehouse district, and lively nightlife.

Scenic Regions

Beyond the cities, Germany's landscapes offer breathtaking beauty and opportunities for outdoor adventure.

  • The Romantic Road: A famous scenic drive through a string of picturesque medieval towns and castles in Bavaria.
  • The Black Forest: Known for its dense forests, charming villages, and cuckoo clocks, it's a paradise for hikers.
  • The Rhine Valley: A UNESCO World Heritage site, famous for its vineyards, hilltop castles, and scenic river cruises.

Cultural Highlights

Germany's cultural calendar is packed with events and traditions that are a must-see for any visitor.

  • Visit the magical Christmas markets in December for festive food and crafts.
  • Explore the fairytale Neuschwanstein Castle, the inspiration for Disney's Sleeping Beauty castle.
  • Enjoy the world's largest beer festival, Oktoberfest, in Munich.

Festive City Breaks

Immerse yourself in the holiday spirit with our festive city breaks. Experience the magic of Europe's most captivating cities, adorned with twinkling lights, bustling Christmas markets, and an air of celebration.

Imperial Vienna, Austria

Experience the grandeur of the former Habsburg capital during the holidays. From imperial palaces hosting elegant balls to world-famous Christmas markets, Vienna is a city of timeless festive elegance.

  • Visit the magical Christmas markets at Schönbrunn Palace and City Hall Square.
  • Attend a New Year's concert featuring the music of Mozart and Strauss.
  • Indulge in holiday pastries at a traditional, cozy Viennese coffee house.

Historic Berlin, Germany

A city of contrasts, Berlin transforms into a winter wonderland with dozens of unique Christmas markets, festive light displays, and a vibrant New Year's Eve party at the Brandenburg Gate.

  • Explore diverse Christmas markets, from the traditional Gendarmenmarkt to the chic market at Charlottenburg Palace.
  • Witness the spectacular New Year's Eve fireworks at the Brandenburg Gate.
  • Enjoy ice skating at Potsdamer Platz and explore the festive illuminations.

Cosmopolitan Zurich, Switzerland

Zurich combines a beautifully preserved old town with a chic, modern holiday atmosphere. Discover unique indoor Christmas markets, fondue chalets, and sparkling lights along the Limmat River.

  • Visit Europe's largest indoor Christmas market inside the main train station.
  • Enjoy a "Singing Christmas Tree" performance and the "Wienachtsdorf" holiday village.
  • Indulge in a traditional cheese fondue in a pop-up winter chalet.

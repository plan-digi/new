About DACH Eve Holidays

DACH Eve Holidays is your expert guide to the heart of Europe. We are a team of passionate travel specialists and local guides dedicated to sharing the rich culture, stunning landscapes, and hidden gems of Germany, Austria, and Switzerland.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create seamless, authentic, and unforgettable holiday experiences. We go beyond the typical tourist trails to connect you with the true character of the DACH region, from its imperial cities to its charming alpine villages.

Our Approach

We believe in quality, authenticity, and personalized service. Our approach is built on three key principles:

  • Local Expertise: All our guides are locals who are passionate about their region and eager to share their insider knowledge with you.
  • Curated Experiences: We meticulously hand-pick hotels, restaurants, and activities to ensure every part of your journey meets our high standards of quality and authenticity.
  • Small Groups: We keep our tour groups small to ensure a personal, flexible, and engaging experience for every traveler, allowing for deeper connections and more memorable moments.

Our Commitment

We are committed to providing exceptional service from the moment you contact us until you return home. We want you to leave with a profound appreciation for the DACH region and a collection of stories to last a lifetime.

Switzerland Winter Wonderland

Experience the breathtaking beauty of Switzerland transformed into a pristine winter wonderland. From snow-covered peaks and charming festive towns to cozy traditions, our holiday tours offer an unforgettable Swiss experience.

Snow-Covered Alpine Peaks

The Swiss Alps are a paradise for winter enthusiasts. Enjoy world-class skiing, snowboarding, and winter hiking amidst some of the most spectacular mountain scenery on Earth.

  • Ski or snowboard in iconic resorts like Zermatt, Verbier, or St. Moritz.
  • Take a cable car to the "Top of Europe" at Jungfraujoch for panoramic views.
  • Enjoy a serene snowshoe hike through pristine alpine forests.
  • Experience the thrill of tobogganing down a mountain slope.

Charming Christmas Towns

Switzerland's towns and cities sparkle with festive cheer during the holidays. Explore enchanting Christmas markets, enjoy ice skating, and soak in the cozy atmosphere.

  • Visit the Montreux Noël Christmas Market on the shores of Lake Geneva.
  • Explore the medieval charm of Lucerne's old town and its festive decorations.
  • Discover Zurich's unique indoor Christmas market at the main train station.
  • Enjoy a festive stroll along the illuminated Bahnhofstrasse in Zurich.

Panoramic Winter Train Journeys

Experience the magic of Switzerland's winter landscapes from the comfort of a panoramic train. These iconic journeys offer unparalleled views of snow-covered mountains, frozen lakes, and charming villages.

  • Ride the legendary Glacier Express, "the slowest express train in the world."
  • Experience the breathtaking Bernina Express, a UNESCO World Heritage route.
  • Enjoy the GoldenPass Line, connecting central Switzerland to Lake Geneva.

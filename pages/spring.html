Winter Alpine Holidays

Experience the magic of a winter wonderland with our curated Alpine holiday tours. From thrilling ski runs to cozy, snow-dusted villages, a festive adventure awaits at every turn.

Festive Alpine Villages

Discover storybook villages blanketed in snow, their streets twinkling with holiday lights. We'll guide you to the most charming spots for festive cheer and local traditions.

  • Stroll through the car-free streets of Zermatt with views of the Matterhorn.
  • Experience the authentic Tyrolean charm of Alpbach, Austria.
  • Explore charming Bavarian villages near Germany's highest peak, the Zugspitze.

Winter Sports Getaways

From world-class ski resorts to serene cross-country trails, the Alps are the ultimate destination for winter sports. We offer holiday packages for all skill levels.

  • Ski or snowboard in famous resorts like St. Anton (Austria) or Zermatt (Switzerland).
  • Enjoy festive tobogganing runs, guided snowshoeing, and scenic winter hikes.
  • Relax in a cozy mountain hut with mulled wine and traditional alpine cuisine.

Scenic Drives & Train Journeys

Watch a winter wonderland unfold from the warmth of a panoramic train. Our scenic rail journeys offer the most comfortable way to witness the dramatic, snow-covered landscapes of the Alps.

  • Ride the world-famous Glacier Express through the heart of the Swiss Alps.
  • Experience the breathtaking Bernina Express as it crosses stunning viaducts.
  • Enjoy cozy, scenic train rides connecting Germany, Austria, and Switzerland.

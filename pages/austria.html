Austria Festive Experiences

Immerse yourself in the imperial grandeur and festive charm of Austria during the holiday season. From Vienna's elegant Christmas markets to Salzburg's Advent magic and the snow-covered Alps, a truly enchanting experience awaits.

Imperial Christmas in Vienna

Vienna transforms into a fairytale city during Advent. Explore numerous Christmas markets, enjoy classical concerts, and experience the opulent decorations of imperial palaces.

  • Wander through the Christkindlmarkt in front of City Hall and the market at Schönbrunn Palace.
  • Attend a festive classical music concert or a New Year's Eve gala.
  • Enjoy a horse-drawn carriage ride through the festively lit streets.

Salzburg's Silent Night & Advent Magic

The birthplace of Mozart and "Silent Night," Salzburg offers a particularly atmospheric Advent season. Its charming old town and fortress provide a stunning backdrop for festive traditions.

  • Visit the historic Salzburg Christmas Market at the foot of Hohensalzburg Fortress.
  • Explore the Silent Night Chapel in Oberndorf, just outside Salzburg.
  • Enjoy traditional Austrian carols and festive performances.

Tyrolean Winter Charm

The Austrian Alps offer a perfect blend of thrilling winter sports and cozy, traditional holiday experiences. Discover picturesque villages and stunning snow-covered landscapes.

  • Ski or snowboard in world-class resorts like St. Anton or Kitzbühel.
  • Experience traditional Tyrolean Advent customs and local crafts.
  • Relax in a warm mountain hut with hearty Austrian cuisine and Glühwein.

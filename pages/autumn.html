Holiday Traditions & Castles

Journey through a rich tapestry of holiday traditions, from magical Christmas markets to fairytale castles adorned in festive splendor. Our holiday cultural trips immerse you in the heart and soul of the season.

Fairytale Castles & Imperial Palaces

Step into a winter storybook as you explore magnificent castles and palaces decorated for the holidays. From the snow-dusted spires of Neuschwanstein to the imperial grandeur of Vienna's Hofburg, these tours are a journey through a festive past.

  • Visit Germany's Neuschwanstein Castle, especially magical when covered in snow.
  • Explore the festive imperial apartments of the Hofburg Palace in Vienna.
  • Discover the historic Chillon Castle on Lake Geneva, set against a winter backdrop.

Festive Music & Concerts

The holiday season comes alive with the sound of music. Our tours offer access to world-class concert halls and opera houses for special holiday performances, from Advent concerts to New Year's galas.

  • Attend a magical Advent or Christmas concert in Vienna or Salzburg.
  • Experience a New Year's Eve gala performance at a historic opera house.
  • Listen to carolers and brass bands at local Christmas markets.

Local Traditions & Festivals

No holiday trip is complete without a visit to the world-famous Christmas markets. Experience the sights, sounds, and smells of this cherished tradition, with handcrafted gifts, delicious treats, and festive music.

  • Explore Nuremberg's Christkindlesmarkt, one of Germany's oldest and most famous.
  • Discover the charm of Vienna's many markets, each with a unique theme.
  • Sip on mulled wine (Glühwein) and shop for unique, handcrafted ornaments.
